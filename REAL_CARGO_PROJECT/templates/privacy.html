Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, use our services, or communicate with us.

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Location data (GPS coordinates) for tracking deliveries
  • Driver documentation (license, identification)
  • Payment information and transaction history

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Connecting customers with drivers
  • Real-time tracking of cargo
  • Processing payments and preventing fraud
  • Communicating with you about your account and services

3. Sharing of Information

We share information between customers and drivers as necessary to complete deliveries. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information.

5. Your Choices

You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging into your account settings.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@cargofind.cm.
